Our Story

We met at a record store, Used Kids Records, after connecting on match.com. We scouted out mowtown and Lou Reed albums and finished the night with hot drinks at Stauf's in Grandview. After a second date that started with a bike ride to the Columbus Museum of Art, we were both intrigued and having a great time sharing laughs, interests, and activities. Our relationship continued as we spent time getting to know each other, meeting friends and families, shopping for more records, eating lots of good food, and going on adventures (Pacific Northwest, French Canada, Puerto Rico). Loriana is learning to golf, and tell jokes. Eric is learning to cook, and play tennis. We are both honing our scuba diving skills.

On a beautiful sunny day in February, we went for a walk at the Scioto Audubon Metro Park. While Loriana was admiring the view of the Columbus Skyline, Eric got down on one knee and... He may not have finished the complete question before Loriana excitedly said, "yes!"
